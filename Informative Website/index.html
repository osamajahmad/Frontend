Welcome Gamers!

This site is a fan-made website about Fortnite. For ISTE-140 Project 1.


Fortnite is a widely popular online video game created by Epic Games Fortnite, first released in 2017. The game quickly became a cultural phenomenon, especially known for its flagship mode, Fortnite Battle Royale. This mode pits 100 players against each other on a large map, where the last player or team standing wins. It combines shooting, building, and strategy elements, making it unique compared to other battle royale games.


Fortnite is available on multiple platforms, including PC, consoles (PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch), and mobile devices. The game is free-to-play, with revenue generated through in-game purchases such as skins, emotes, and battle passes that offer cosmetic items and challenges.


Fortnite is also known for its frequent updates and seasonal events, which introduce new content, themes, and gameplay mechanics. The game has a vibrant community and has become a significant part of popular culture, influencing music, sports, and entertainment. Overall, Fortnite's blend of fast-paced action, creative building mechanics, and social interaction has made it a lasting favorite in the gaming world.
